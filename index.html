Who We Are

A Space for Ideas,
Learning & Real Work

AI4Botswana is more than a project — it's a movement. We exist to make sure Botswana isn't just a consumer of AI, but a creator of it. Locally rooted, globally connected.

We connect startups with investors, researchers with communities, and innovators with opportunity — all under the guidance of PulaSpace.
